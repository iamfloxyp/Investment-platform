Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. Learn how we handle and protect your data at Emuntra.

1. Introduction

At Emuntra, we value your privacy and are committed to safeguarding your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you visit our platform or use our services.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information from you:

  • Personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, and country.
  • Account information including wallet addresses, deposit history, and investment preferences.
  • Technical data like browser type, IP address, and device information for security and analytics.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information helps us to:

  • Provide, operate, and improve our services.
  • Verify your identity and process deposits or withdrawals securely.
  • Communicate updates, investment notifications, and account-related information.
  • Comply with regulatory and anti-fraud requirements.

4. Data Security

We implement strong encryption and security protocols to ensure your data is safe. All sensitive information, including passwords, is encrypted and never shared with third parties without consent.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell or trade your personal data. However, we may share information with trusted partners such as payment processors or regulatory authorities when legally required.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information. You can also request that we stop processing your data or send a copy of your information upon request.

7. Cookies

Emuntra uses cookies to enhance user experience, remember preferences, and analyze traffic. You can manage cookie settings through your browser at any time.

8. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect new regulations or operational changes. The latest version will always be available on this page.

9. Contact Us

For questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, contact us at hello@emuntra.com.
